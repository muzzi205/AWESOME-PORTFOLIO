Dangers of Drug Abuse

Drug abuse affects not just individuals, but entire families and communities. It clouds judgment, creates addiction, and often ends lives prematurely.

"For this generation, my biggest fear is drug abuse." — My father's lecture at Unilorin Secondary School

Young people often fall into this trap out of curiosity or peer pressure. But curiosity can be fatal — and addiction can begin with just one bad decision. Prevention is the key.

We must educate and intervene early. Parents need to stay involved. Teachers must speak out. Friends need to support, not enable. Society must step in.

Books like “Addiction” by Nicole Bertram help us understand the science and psychology behind dependency. Knowledge saves lives. Awareness builds hope.

Change begins with you. Whether you raise your voice, support someone silently, or share resources — your action matters.
